Dear Sir/Madam,

Greetings from Tare Zameen Foundation!

We are a non-profit organization, set up in the year 2018 in Delhi. Since day one, our objective has been the upliftment of children, aged citizens, divyangs and women, education for the underprivileged children, and helping with employment opportunities for women. We have been driving various campaigns and projects to serve our mission. Our recent project - 'Meri Pehchan' - involved education awareness and book distribution drive among poor children and early child education.

Imagine when we were children, the festive season of Diwali signified the ultimate period of fun and happiness. As a parent, one wishes to give that love and joy to their children. But can you imagine the hopelessness and despair felt by countless innocent souls this year when their financial conditions prevent them from being able to get a new pair of clothes, much less, light a single diya?

To help these underprivileged families, we at the Tare Zameen Foundation have planned an 'Ummeed ki Diwali' event.

This festive season, we plan to provide Ummeed ki Diwali (east costing around Rs. 2600/-per family) to 420 underprivileged families in South Delhi who are adversely affected by Covid . We cannot effectively do so without the help and generosity shown by honourable institutions like yours. We will be delighted to have your organization join us for this humble initiative to spread happiness among these families.

Please find attached the Association Support Proposal along with our Brochure, which has detailed information about our organization. We will be happy to address any questions that you may have about our organization and the Diwali event. We look forward to your positive response.
